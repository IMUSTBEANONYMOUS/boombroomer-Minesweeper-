How to play?

In this game u have to click and reveal a tile, Avoid revealing a tile with a mine. To check if there is mine check the numbers given n some tiles to identify them. Flag all the mines before stepping on one. Good luck.

How to calculate tiles?

If your lucky and u step on a tile without a mine then u might get a number below it. The number states that out of the eight numbers it has there are those number of tiles in its neighbours. Use this information to flag the tiles.

How you might lose?

If u click on a tile with a mine. Then ur out. Or if u run out of flags , ur not technically out and probably u have flagged the wrong tile so crosscheck it.
